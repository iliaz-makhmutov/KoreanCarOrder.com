It is named after the city of Santa Fe, New Mexico, and was introduced for the 2001 model year as Hyundai's first SUV.

The Santa Fe was a milestone in the company's restructuring program of the late 1990s because the SUV was a hit with American buyers. .

Between 2006 and 2012, the Santa Fe was positioned between the smaller Tucson compact crossover and the larger Veracruz.
